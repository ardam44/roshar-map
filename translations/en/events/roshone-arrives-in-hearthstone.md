# Roshone arrives in Hearthstone

Exiled from Kholinar by Dalinar, Roshone arrived as citylord of Hearthstone.

## Details

Roshone was placed as the citylord of Hearthstone. Upon his arrival, Roshone showed no respect for the inhabitants, and appeared particularly distasteful of Lirin, whom he blamed for the death of the previous citylord.
